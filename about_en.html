About me

Hello! My name is Nadal and I'm a Data Science student at the Polytechnic University of Valencia. I am passionate for finding patterns in data, building predicting models and getting valuable information out of real circumstances.

I consider myself a curious, dedicated and creative person, with a high learning and adatping capability. I thrive in team projects in leading positions and facing individual challenges with determination. I have practical experience in Python, C++, R, SQL, HTML, CSS, Power BI and IBM Granite, which let me develop innovative and comprehensible solutions out of complex data.

My desire for improvement drives me to constantly take part in hackathons and competitive programming contests, where I can apply my skills in real-world scenarios and learn from other profesionals in the sector. I enjoy solving complex problems and creating efficient algorithms.

Appart from technology, I have a passion for music and sports. I sing as a tenor at the Orfeó Universitari de València and enjoy doing martial arts such as judo, which help me to find balance and find personal improvements that give value to my everyday and profesional life.

I am open to new opportunities, collaborations, and exciting projects. Feel free to reach out if you want to connect.
